How your data becomes model parameters

Compute inter-arrival times (IAT) — differences between consecutive arrival timestamps

Mean and standard deviation of IAT → CVₐ = σ(IAT) / μ(IAT)
CVₐ = σ / μ

Mean and standard deviation of service times → CVₛ = σ(S) / μ(S)
CVₛ = σ / μ
